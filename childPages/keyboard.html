Keyboard Controls










  • Esc: Start menu is where you can get to the settings or leave game and stuff like that.

  • Tab: Scoreboard is where you can see your enemys, your teammates and the scores of everyone.

  • Shift: Sprint makes you run faster.

  • Ctrl: Prone makes your character lay on the floor.

  • 1: Primary weapon is your main gun like ar, shotgun etc.

  • 2: Secondary weapon is your pistol or smg 12 etc.

  • 5: Observation tool is the button you click to use your bullet proof cam etc.(if ur player has the item.

  • 6: This is how you put done your drone.

  • W: Move forward.

  • S: Move backwards.

  • A: Move left.

  • D: Move right.

  • Z: Ping will show a yellow dot thing where ever u want to ping or you can ping items/gadgets.

  • X: Enemy scan is when you are on your drone it will identify what operator the enemy is when u scan.

  • B: Switch firing mode so for example you go from full auto to birst fire.

  • Space Bar: Will make you jump over things that are around the maps when you are close enough to the thing.

  • Q: Lean left makes you lean to the left when you aim or when you are not aiming.

  • E: Lean right makes you lean to the right when you aim or when you are not aiming.

  • R: This will reload your gun.

  • F: Interact will let you put gadgets down, baracde dorrs etc.

  • G: Genaric gadgets are the deployable sheilds, barbed wire, soft breach etc.

  • C: Crouch will put your character in a crouch position.

  • V: Melee will make your character hit the enemy for a instant kill if you hit them.

  • Left Click: This will make your gun shoot.

  • Right Click: This will make you aim in.

  • Scroll Wheele Button: Secial ability is a ability only that operator has. Each operator has a special ability.
