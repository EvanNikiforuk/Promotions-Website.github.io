Rainbow Six Siege Modes


Modes

  • Every mode is a 5 vs 5. You are deffending or attacking a bomb

  • Deffending: You can renforce site "A" and "B". You can also use your operators special abilitys to help.

  • Attacking: You pick an operator. You get into drone phase where you have to find site "A" and "B". then you go and attack.

  • Ranked: Ranked is 8 rounds at most. Ranked is a mode that you play 10 matches at the start of every season and after those 10 matches depending on how you did you get placed in a rank. Every game you win you get points to move up a rank and every loss you lose points to bring you down. You can't leave this mode or you will be banned.

  • Unranked: Unranked is 8 rounds max. There is nothing special with unranked. You can't leave this mode or you will get banned>

  • Quick Match: Quick match is 5 rounds at most. There is nothing special about quick match. You can leave this mode mid game and you won't get banned.

  • Training Grounds: This is where you can get waves of bots vs you, you can do you vs many bots and you go till you kill all or you die, or there is a shooting range.

  • Learning Area: This is basicly a tutorial.

  • Custom Game: This is quick match but filled only with the people in your party.5 vs 5.

  • Ever so often there will be a special game mode that is only in the modes for a limited time.
