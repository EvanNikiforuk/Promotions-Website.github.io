How To Win






  • 3 Lines Button: Start menu is where you can get to the settings or leave game and stuff like that.

  • 2 Square Button: Scoreboard is where you can see your enemys, your teammates and the scores of everyone.

  • Click L3: Sprint makes you run faster.

  • Hold B: Prone makes your character lay on the floor.

  • Y: Primary weapon is your main gun like ar, shotgun etc.

  • Y: Secondary weapon is your pistol or smg 12 etc.

  • Down On D-Pad: Observation tool is the button you click to use your bullet proof cam etc.(if ur player has the item.

  • Right On D-Pad: This is how you put done your drone.

  • L3: Move forward.

  • L3: Move backwards.

  • L3: Move left.

  • L3: Move right.

  • Left On D-Pad: Ping will show a yellow dot thing where ever u want to ping or you can ping items/gadgets.

  • Hold Y: Enemy scan is when you are on your drone it will identify what operator the enemy is when u scan.

  • Up On D-Pad: Switch firing mode so for example you go from full auto to birst fire.

  • A: Will make you jump over things that are around the maps when you are close enough to the thing.

  • Click L3: Lean left makes you lean to the left when you aim in.(ONLY WHEN YOU AIM IN)

  • Click R3: Lean right makes you lean to the right when you aim in.(ONLY WHEN YOU AIM IN)

  • X: This will reload your gun.

  • Hold X: Interact will let you put gadgets down, baracde dorrs etc.

  • Lb: Genaric gadgets are the deployable sheilds, barbed wire, soft breach etc.

  • Click B: Crouch will put your character in a crouch position.

  • Click R3: Melee will make your character hit the enemy for a instant kill if you hit them.

  • Rt: This will make your gun shoot.

  • Lt: This will make you aim in.

  • Rb: Secial ability is a ability only that operator has. Each operator has a special ability.

  • R3: This is how you look around
