  • You have the opption to change your scope, grip, muzzle, and the laser

  • Your grip is where one of your hands will go. This might change the recoil pattern a bit depending what grip you choose.

  • Your muzzle is the thing at the end of your weapon. This might change the damage or recoil of the gun.

  • The laser is like a laser pointer at the end of your gun. The enemys can see the laser.
